Privacy Policy

Blank2Bite - Fishing Journal App

Effective Date: February 15, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Blank2Bite ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Blank2Bite is a fishing journal application that enables users to record catches with photographs, AI-powered species identification, meteorological data, and geographic location tracking.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

To provide our services, we require user authentication. We collect the following information depending on your sign-in method:

  • Google Sign-In: email address associated with your Google account
  • Email/Password Authentication: email address and securely encrypted password

All authentication is processed through Google Firebase Authentication, a secure, industry-standard authentication service.

2.2 Catch Records

When you log a fishing catch, the App records the following information:

  • Fish species and bait/lure type (identified via AI or entered manually by the user)
  • Estimated fish size
  • Date and time of capture
  • Geographic coordinates (derived from photo EXIF metadata or device GPS)
  • Meteorological conditions at the time and location of capture
  • Lunar phase and illumination data
  • Marine conditions, including wave height and sea surface temperature

2.3 Photographs

The App requests access to your device camera and photo library to capture or select images of your catches.

Important: Photographs are stored exclusively on your device. They are never uploaded to our servers for permanent storage. Images are temporarily transmitted in compressed form to our backend server solely for AI-powered species identification and are immediately discarded upon completion of the analysis.

2.4 Location Data

With your explicit permission, the App collects geographic coordinates for the following purposes:

  • Recording the precise location of each catch
  • Retrieving historical weather and marine conditions for that location
  • Displaying catch locations on an interactive map within the App

Location data is obtained either from EXIF metadata embedded in photographs or from your device's GPS sensor.

3. How We Use Your Information

We process your data exclusively for the purposes outlined below. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Type Purpose Processed By

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All catch data and photographs are stored locally on your device using an encrypted SQLite database. The App is designed to function fully offline without an internet connection.

4.2 Cloud Synchronization

Catch records (textual data only; photographs are excluded) are synchronized to Google Cloud Firestore to enable backup and multi-device access. Your data is stored within your personal, isolated account. Firebase security rules enforce strict access control, ensuring that only you can read and modify your own data.

4.3 Backend Infrastructure

Our backend server functions exclusively as a secure proxy for third-party API services. No user data is persistently stored on our servers. Photographs transmitted for AI analysis are processed entirely in memory and are purged immediately upon completion.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication & Cloud Firestore — Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini AI — Fish species identification — Terms of Service
  • Open-Meteo — Meteorological data (open-source) — Terms of Use
  • Google Play Billing — In-app subscription management — Privacy Policy

6. Device Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions to deliver its core functionality:

  • Camera — Required to photograph your catches directly within the App
  • Photos & Media — Required to select existing images from your device gallery
  • Location — Required to geotag catches and retrieve location-based weather data
  • Internet — Required for AI identification, weather data retrieval, cloud synchronization, and user authentication

All permissions are requested at runtime and may be individually granted or denied. The App will continue to operate with reduced functionality if specific permissions are not granted.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Your data is retained for the duration of your active account
  • You may delete individual catch records at any time from within the App
  • Deleted records are permanently removed from both local storage and cloud synchronization
  • To request complete deletion of all account data, please contact us at the address provided below

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take immediate steps to delete it.
