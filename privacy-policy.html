Blank2Bite - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Blank2Bite ("we", "our", "the app") is a fishing journal app that helps anglers record their catches with photos, AI-powered fish identification, weather data, and GPS location. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you sign in, we collect:

  • Google Sign-In: your email address (provided by Google)
  • Email/Password: your email address and an encrypted password

Authentication is handled by Google Firebase Authentication.

2.2 Fishing Catch Data

When you record a catch, the following data is stored:

  • Fish species (identified by AI or entered manually)
  • Bait/lure type (identified by AI or entered manually)
  • Estimated fish size
  • Date and time of the catch
  • GPS coordinates (from photo EXIF data or device location)
  • Weather conditions at the time of catch
  • Moon phase
  • Marine conditions (wave height, sea temperature)

2.3 Photos

The app accesses your device camera and photo gallery to capture or select photos of your catches. Photos are:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Not uploaded to our servers for storage
  • Temporarily sent (compressed) to our backend server solely for AI fish identification, and immediately discarded after processing

2.4 Location Data

The app collects GPS coordinates to:

  • Record where you caught the fish
  • Retrieve weather and marine conditions for that location
  • Display catch locations on a map

Location is obtained from photo EXIF metadata or from your device's GPS sensor (with your permission).

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All catch data and photos are stored locally on your device in an SQLite database. The app works fully offline.

4.2 Cloud Storage

Catch records (text data only, not photos) are synced to Google Firebase Firestore for backup and multi-device access. Data is stored in your personal account and is not accessible by other users. Firebase security rules ensure that only you can read and write your own data.

4.3 Backend Server

Our backend server (hosted on Railway) acts as a proxy for external APIs. It does not store any user data permanently. Photos sent for AI identification are processed in memory and immediately discarded.

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore) - Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini AI (fish identification via our backend) - Terms of Service
  • Open-Meteo (weather data, open source) - Terms
  • Google Play Billing (in-app subscriptions) - Privacy Policy

6. Permissions

The app requests the following Android permissions:

  • Camera: to take photos of your catches
  • Photos/Media: to select photos from your gallery
  • Location: to record catch location and retrieve weather data
  • Internet: for AI identification, weather data, cloud sync, and authentication

All permissions are optional. The app will work with reduced functionality if permissions are denied.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Your data is retained as long as you have an active account
  • You can delete individual catches from within the app at any time
  • To delete all your data, you can contact us at blank2bite@gmail.com
  • Deleted data is removed from both local storage and cloud sync

8. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
