How to Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your Blank2Bite account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

Steps to request account deletion:

  1. Send an email to blank2bite@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. In the email body, confirm that you want to permanently delete your account and all associated data
  4. We will process your request and confirm the deletion within 7 business days
Request Account Deletion

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon processing your deletion request, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account information — Your authentication credentials and email address
  • Catch records — All fishing catch data stored in the cloud (species, location, weather, dates)
  • Cloud-synced data — All records stored in Google Cloud Firestore
Please note: Photographs are stored exclusively on your device and are not part of cloud storage. Deleting your account does not remove photos from your device. You can delete them manually from your device's gallery.

Delete Individual Records

If you only want to delete specific catch records without closing your account, you can do so directly within the App:

  1. Open the Blank2Bite app
  2. Go to the History tab
  3. Swipe or tap the delete icon on the catch you want to remove
  4. Confirm the deletion

Deleted records are permanently removed from both your device and cloud storage.
